AI Personalities

Cowardly Tank

Tank patrols around waypoints, then flees when the player is below 50 health.


Aggressive Tank

Tank Charges player with intent to kill, letting nothing get in its way... once obstacle avoidance is fixed.


Turret Tank

Tank patrols like a cowardly tank, however rotates towards a sound, then aims at player if seen and 
shoots when aimed correctly. For now it shows aiming and firing along with patrolling, 
this is due to issues with the new hearing and seeing components. 


Soldier Tank

A strong and sturdy Tank, will not flee from battle. Follows the player at a distance to try and eliminate them.
