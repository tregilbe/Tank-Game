AI Personalities

Cowardly Tank

Tank patrols around waypoints, then flees when their health is below 50.


Aggressive Tank

Tank Charges player with intent to kill, letting nothing get in its way.


Turret Tank

Tank stays in one spot, rotating and checking for the enemy, if seen the player is shot at.

Scout Tank

Tank patrols like a cowardly tank, however rotates towards a sound, then aims at player if seen and 
shoots when aimed correctly.
